AEPS Services

Aadhaar Enabled Payment System also known as AEPS in short is basically a new payment service provided by the NPCI to banks and financial companies using ‘Aadhaar card’. Aadhaar is issued by UIDAI (Unique Identification Authority of India). It is a unique identification number of every individual in India. AEPS API service can be used at CSPs (Customer Service Points) maintained by some Business Correspondents (BCs) using POS devices. Softcare Infotech is one of the best AEPS API service providers that provide AEPS services across all over India.
